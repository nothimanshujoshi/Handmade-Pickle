A Taste of the Himalayas

At Bhukripa Farm, nestled in the pristine valleys of Dehradun, we believe in the magic of slow food. Our journey began with a simple mission: to preserve the authentic, homemade recipes passed down through generations.

We don't just make pickles; we nurture them. Using 100% farm-fresh ingredients grown without harmful chemicals, our traditional pickling process ensures every bite is bursting with purity, health, and unmatched flavor. No factories, no shortcuts—just pure tradition.
